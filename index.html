About Stirling Interiors

_____________

Amazing workspaces don’t just happen – getting it right takes time and thought. Stirling Interiors have the benefit of over 40 years’ experience in developing effective, commercial fit-out and refurbishment solutions .

Our expert designers and projects team will help you to improve and refurbish your existing or new workspace. Whether it’s commercial office space or other commercial premises, we will tailor our refurbishment services to suit, giving your company its very own ‘workspace facelift’ to help your business grow to new heights.

Delighting clients is what drives us. Be it a new or old building to refurbish; office or educational; our enthusiastic team of fit-out specialists will partner with you to provide the very best results for your business. On-time. In-budget. And always working around your individual needs. We deliver full ‘turn-key’ refurbishment projects that result in inspiring and engaging places to work.

We’re big players in small refurbishment works too. Our many smaller scale successes stem from effective client liaison, delivering tailor-made solutions with a high-speed turnaround. In fact, if you’re looking to design, re-design or update any commercial space, Stirling Interiors have the know-how.

Why Choose Us?

_____________

Here at Stirling Interiors we care about our customers and our reputation within the commercial fit-out industry. Most of our new enquiries come from recommendations from existing customers which we feel speaks volumes for the quality of our refurbishment works.

We like to listen to our customers and gain a real sense of what they want to achieve should this be a simple partition installed out of hours or a full office refurbishment that is phased over a few months. We understand that businesses need to keep running whilst refurbishment works take place and we liaise with you to ensure that this happens and that the day-to-day running of your business can continue smoothly.

We at Stirling Interiors are proud of the commercial fit-outs that we complete and we want more than anything for our customers to be over the moon too, we finish jobs properly and to a high standard, we are a friendly, approachable bunch and build great relationships with our customers, we would love you to be one of them!

FULL INDUSTRY ACCREDITATIONS

Having access to a wide selection of product ranges all designed for commercial environments and supplied with the appropriate certifications and warranties, enable Stirling Interiors to source the correct products for each refurbishment project should it be fabrics that are bleachable, flooring for partially sighted people or the latest trends in office, educational or healthcare furniture; Stirling will be able to source the correct solution.

FRIENDLY APPROACHABLE TEAM

Stirling Interiors has a team of designers, project managers, contractors and site managers to ensure your refurbishment project runs as smoothly as possible. Between us we have years of experience within the industry, a vast array of qualifications and we all like to do a job thoroughly and to the clients satisfaction.

OVER 40 YEARS’ EXPERIENCE

Established in 1975 Stirling Interiors has been refurbishing commercial spaces for quite some time! Trends have changed over the years, some for the better and some for the worse but the main principles of commercial fit-outs has remained the same and that is to refurbish workspaces to make the most of the space, to meet the clients goals and to create as minimum disruption as possible.
